Genshin Impact 4.5 Update: New Characters and Spring Festival Event

By Gacha Team | March 31, 2025

The *Genshin Impact* 4.5 update, released on March 31, 2025, brings a fresh wave of content for players. Headlining the update are two new characters: a 5-star Hydro sword user, Liana, and a 4-star Geo polearm user, Kael. Liana’s abilities focus on rapid Hydro slashes with a unique elemental burst that summons a tidal wave, while Kael provides Geo shields and boosts team defense with his elemental skill.

Alongside the new characters, the Spring Festival event introduces a limited-time banner featuring Liana and a rerun of the popular 5-star character, Albedo. The event also includes a new storyline set in Mondstadt, where players can participate in a flower festival, complete mini-games, and earn exclusive rewards like a 4-star weapon, the "Blossom Blade." Additionally, the event offers double drop rates for certain domains, making it an ideal time for players to farm resources.

The update also brings quality-of-life improvements, such as an enhanced artifact sorting system and a new co-op mode for the Spiral Abyss, allowing players to team up for floors 9-12. However, some players have reported issues with the new co-op mode, including lag during high-intensity battles. The developers at HoYoverse have promised a patch to address these concerns in the coming weeks.

Overall, the 4.5 update has been well-received by the community, with many praising the new characters’ designs and the festive atmosphere of the Spring Festival. As *Genshin Impact* continues to expand its roster and events, players are already looking forward to what the 4.6 update will bring.

Honkai: Star Rail 2.1 Anniversary: New Banners and Rewards

By Gacha Team | March 30, 2025

*Honkai: Star Rail* celebrated its second anniversary with the 2.1 update on March 30, 2025, introducing a slew of new content for players. The update features two new 5-star characters: Seraphina, a Quantum-type damage dealer, and Milo, an Imaginary-type support. Seraphina’s kit revolves around stacking Quantum damage over time, while Milo enhances team energy regeneration and provides buffs to Imaginary damage.

The anniversary event, "Stellar Celebration," offers players 20 free pulls on a special banner featuring Seraphina and a rerun of fan-favorite character Jing Yuan. Players can also earn up to 1,600 Stellar Jades by completing event missions, which include a new boss battle against a massive Abundant Ebon Deer. The event also introduces a limited-time game mode, "Memory of Chaos: Anniversary Edition," with increased difficulty and exclusive rewards like a 5-star light cone, "Eternal Harmony."

In addition to the event, the update includes a permanent addition to the game: a "Memory Capsule" system that allows players to replay past event stories. This has been a highly requested feature, as many players missed out on earlier events. The community has largely praised the anniversary update, though some have noted that the new boss battle is particularly challenging for free-to-play players.
